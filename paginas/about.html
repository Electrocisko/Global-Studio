Who's behind Global Studio?
I am Paola Llantada, an architect influenced by all the new emerging businesses and entrepreneurs who are committed to making a large commercial impact. My inspiration comes from helping to promote small and large businesses by designing and/or redesigning their commercial spaces by implementing high-quality design and innovation that aims to reflect their values through their interior design and architecture. Furthermore, ensuring that they are capable of generating a purposeful and memorable buyer experience that values the needs and wants of those who visit their brick-and-mortar.

WHAT´S OUR PROCESS?

1. GETTING TO KNOW YOUR BUSINESS
We discuss your goals and the values that drive your business in detail. We dissect and analyze their core meaning to capture their significance in a physical space.
2. BRAINSTORMING
Creative process where the concept of your business is proposed and/or reinforced. We analyze commercial trends and competitors within your market to ensure your project is unique and notable.
3. DESIGN & CREATION
The application of our discussions and research. We work on the development of the project and provide regular updates to ensure you are part of the process.
4. MAKE IT REAL
In addition to developing your design strategy, we can also help you build in the physical world. Specialized construction and high-quality materials are essential in order to maximize the success of your project.
